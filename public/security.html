ClipSync Security

Password Protection

ClipSync uses password protection to secure your clipboard data. Your clips are encrypted and stored safely on your local device.

Local Storage Only

All your clipboard data is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to any servers, no cloud sync, and no data collection. Your privacy is guaranteed.

Key Security Features:

  • Password Required: Access to clips requires your password
  • Encrypted Storage: All clips are encrypted on your device
  • Secure Access: No unauthorized access possible
  • Privacy Protection: Complete privacy with offline operation

No Internet Required

ClipSync works completely offline. This means:

  • No data transmission over the internet
  • No servers to store your data
  • No risk of data breaches
  • Complete control over your data

Data Privacy

Since ClipSync is 100% offline:

  • We cannot see your clipboard data
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No user accounts needed
  • No data sharing with third parties

What Happens If You Forget Your Password?

Since data is stored locally with password protection, forgetting your password means you cannot access your encrypted clips. You'll need to reset and start fresh. We recommend using a strong, memorable password.
