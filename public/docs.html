Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is ClipSync?

ClipSync is a 100% free and open source clipboard manager created by Vivek Rawat (RVT). It stores unlimited text clips locally with password protection, search/filter capabilities, and a dark mode interface.

Which platforms does ClipSync support?

ClipSync is available for Windows and Linux only. The app works completely offline and stores all data locally on your device.

Is ClipSync free to use?

Yes! ClipSync is 100% free forever with no subscriptions, no paid plans, and no limitations. All features are included for free.

Security & Privacy

How secure is my clipboard data?

Your clipboard data is protected with password encryption and stored locally on your device. No data is sent to any servers - everything stays on your computer.

Can ClipSync see my clipboard data?

No. ClipSync works completely offline and stores all data locally on your device. There are no servers, no cloud sync, and no data collection.

What happens if I forget my password?

Since data is stored locally with password protection, forgetting your password means you cannot access your encrypted clips. You'll need to reset and start fresh.

Features & Usage

How do I search my clipboard history?

Open ClipSync and use the search bar at the top. Type any text to instantly find clips. You can also use the filter options to show all clips or filter by category.

Can I organize my clipboard items?

ClipSync focuses on simplicity. You can save unlimited text clips, search through them instantly, and delete any clips you no longer need with one-click operations.

How do I copy clips?

Each clip in ClipSync has a one-click copy button. Simply click on any clip to copy it to your clipboard, then paste it anywhere you need it.

Troubleshooting

ClipSync isn't capturing my clipboard

Ensure ClipSync has the necessary permissions. On Windows, try running as administrator if needed. Check that ClipSync is running in the system tray.

The app is running slowly

Large clipboard histories can affect performance. Try deleting old clips you no longer need. Ensure you have enough free storage space on your device.
